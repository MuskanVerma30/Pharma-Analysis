Pharma Analysis Dashboard



**Overview**



This project presents an interactive \*\*Pharma Analysis Dashboard\*\* developed using \*\*SQL\*\* and \*\*Power BI\*\* to analyze healthcare data and provide meaningful insights into patient encounters, medication usage, disease prevalence, and treatment patterns.



The dashboard transforms raw healthcare data into actionable business intelligence, enabling healthcare professionals and decision-makers to monitor key performance indicators, identify trends, and support data-driven clinical decisions.







**Business Objective**



Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of patient and treatment data. The objective of this project is to convert that data into interactive visualizations that help answer critical business questions such as:



\* Which medical conditions are most frequently diagnosed?

\* Which medications are prescribed the most?

\* How does medication usage vary by gender?

\* What are the monthly patient encounter trends?

\* How can healthcare providers use these insights to improve patient care and resource planning?







**Dataset**



The dataset contains healthcare-related information, including:



\* Patient demographics

\* Medical conditions

\* Prescribed medications

\* Treatment duration

\* Patient encounters

\* Admission dates







**Tools \& Technologies**



\* SQL

\* Power BI

\* Data Modeling

\* DAX







**Dashboard Features**



The dashboard provides interactive analysis of:



\* Total Cases

\* Total Medical Conditions

\* Average Medications Prescribed

\* Average Treatment Duration

\* Medication Usage by Gender

\* Top Prescribed Medications

\* Top Medical Conditions

\* Monthly Patient Encounters

\* Patient Distribution by Gender



Users can filter the dashboard by \*\*Year\*\* and \*\*Quarter\*\* to explore trends over different time periods.







**Key Insights**



\* The dashboard tracks \*\*7K patient cases\*\* across \*\*118 medical conditions\*\*.

\* Medication usage is slightly higher among female patients.

\* Viral sinusitis is the most frequently recorded medical condition.

\* Certain medications account for a significant proportion of prescriptions, indicating common treatment patterns.

\* Patient encounters fluctuate throughout the year, with noticeable peaks during specific months.

\* The gender distribution of patients remains relatively balanced.







**Business Value**



This dashboard enables healthcare organizations to:



\* Monitor patient trends through interactive KPIs.

\* Identify the most common medical conditions.

\* Analyze prescription patterns.

\* Support evidence-based healthcare planning.

\* Improve operational and clinical decision-making.
